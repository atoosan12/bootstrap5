All About My Love for Cats

Cats are a very unique species. They're like their own person in a way. They're also super adorable and are just as smart as dogs.

Facts About Cats

Cats have many cool features that help them survive in the world. One cool feature is their eyes. They're able to see in the dark which helps them catch their prey. Another feature is their teeth. Their teeth has three adaptable funtions, stabbing, anchoring, and cutting. Here is a list of some more interesting facts about cats.

  • Cats can jump up to 5 times their height
  • Cats are better hunters than dogs
  • House cats share 95% of their genetic makeup with tigers
  • House cats can reach speeds of 30mph over a short distance
